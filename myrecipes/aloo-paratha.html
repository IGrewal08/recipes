Description

Aloo Paratha is a potato stuffed flatbread indian dish, filled with delicious spices and fillings

So here are is the required ingredients and process you can follow along to make your own aloo paratha!

Ingredients

  • White flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Shallots/Scallions (chopped)
  • Fresh coriander/cilantro
  • Fresh ginger
  • Garam Masala
  • Ajwain/Carom seeds
  • Chili powder
  • Salt

Steps:

  • Spiced potato filling;
    1. Rough-Mash Potatoes into a cream form
    2. Cook spices along with peas, chilli and ginger
    3. Add the mashed potatoes into the fix
    4. Mix fresh coriander and let filling cool off
  • The Aloo Paratha dough;
    1. Place flour, water and salt in bowl and fix together
    2. Once dough is formed work into surface and knead it 60 times until smooth and elastic
    3. Forum dough into a ball, and cover with cling wrap an set aside for 20 minutes
    4. Mix the filling into a bowl and mix and combine the ingredients
  • Shaping the dough;
    1. Preheat the oven to very low
    2. Cut dough into 4 pieces with a knife
    3. Take one ball and use a rolling bin to roll dough into round circle diameter 18cm
    4. Place 1/3 cup of filing into the middle of the rolled dough
    5. gather the edge of the circular dough and enclose the filling (remove as much air as possible)
    6. Flatten the ball slightly and shape dough into a disk
    7. Turn the dough over so enclosed side is facing down, and use a rolling pin to roll out until 1/2 cm thickness
  • Heating;
    1. Heat 1 tbs of oil into a non-stick frying pan over medium heat
    2. Place one Paratha into the frying pan and let sit for 2 minute, once done; flip to the other side
    3. Remove from frying pan onto a wire rack, and place into a low oven to keep the remaining Parathas warm while cooking
    4. Add a small drizzle of remaining oil into the pan and cook the remaining Parathas
    5. Once cooked, cut into 4 small pieces and server
