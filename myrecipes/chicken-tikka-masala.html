Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a popular chicken curry dish, added with a curry sauce, rice and naan flat bread makes it one of the most popular dishes in the world

So here are is the required ingredients and process you can follow along to make your own Chicken Tikka Masala!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • Plain yoghurt
  • Cloves garlic
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Turmeric
  • Coriander
  • Cardamom powder
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps:

  • Spiced potato filling;
    1. Rough-Mash Potatoes into a cream form
    2. Cook spices along with peas, chilli and ginger
    3. Add the mashed potatoes into the fix
    4. Mix fresh coriander and let filling cool off
  • Creating the Chicken Tikka;
    1. Combine all ingredients except chicken into a bowl and mix, add chicken when well mixed
    2. Cover with cling wrap and leave in fridge to marinate overnight
    3. Heat oil in a non stick pan over high heat and add half the chicken and spread out
    4. Scrape out charred bits left in pan and discard
  • Making the Sauce;
    1. Wipe skillet, and place on heat of medium high
    2. Add oil and butter, once melted add onions, ginger, and salt
    3. Cook by stirring constantly to ensure the sauce does't burn, do so for 6 minutes
    4. Add curry Sauce and spices, cook for further 2 minutes while stirring
    5. Add tomato passata and water and mix, bring to a simmer then cover and reduce heat
    6. Let simmer for 15 minutes, with occasional stirring
    7. Pour curry into a bowl and then use a stick bender to puree until smooth
    8. Return sauce to skillet, add cream, sugar, and butter, stir until butter melts
    9. Add chicken to skillet and stir for a few minutes until chicken is cooked through
    10. Server Chicken Tikka Masala over rice and naan flatbread
