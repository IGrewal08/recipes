Description

Samosa is a indian snack, a pyramid shaped, flay pastry, stuffed with delicious spiced potato filling

So here are is the required ingredients and process you can follow along to make your own Samosa!

Ingredients

  • Ajwain seeds
  • Mango powder
  • hing (Asafoetida)
  • Black mustard seeds
  • Garam Masala
  • Cumin seeds and powder
  • Coriander seeds and Turmeric
  • Flour
  • Green chilli
  • Ghee /or Oil
  • Potatoes
  • Ginger
  • Peas
  • Coriander/Cilantro

Steps:

  • Spiced potato filling;
    1. Rough-Mash Potatoes into a cream form
    2. Cook spices along with peas, chilli and ginger
    3. Add the mashed potatoes into the fix
    4. Mix fresh coriander and let filling cool off
  • The Samosa dough;
    1. Mix dry ingredients and add ghee, (flour, seeds and salt)
    2. Rub ghee into dry mix until it resembles dry breadcrumbs
    3. Form dough by adding water (dough should be soft but not too sticky)
    4. Divide the dough by forming it into a log and cutting it into 6 equal pieces
    5. Shape each piece into a ball
    6. Roll out balls into a 2mm think disk
  • Making the Samosa parcels;
    1. Take a knife and cut your disks into half
    2. Brush the outer rim of the disk with water
    3. Fold the half disk into a cone shape
    4. Fill the cone with our spiced potato filling
    5. Brush water around the circular ridge of the cone and seal the cone closed
    6. Trim any extra pastry off
    7. Pinch Top of cone to make it painty and similar to a pyramid
  • Frying;
    1. First heat oil to 160°C/320°F then fry 3/4 samosas for 3 minutes
    2. Once golden brown, take out the samosas and place on a paper towel to drain
    3. Repeat steps while increasing heat oil to 190°C/375°F in 2-3 batches for 2 minutes
    4. Drain samosas on paper towel once golden brown
